Questionnaire transliteration of lafaz Al-Quran

Assalamu'alaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh. This questionnaire is part of a research on lafaz search system in Al-Quran by Billy Zaelani Malik from Computer Science Department, Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences, Bogor Agricultural University. In each section, pieces of verses from the Qur'an are shown in the form of sound files. Please fill in the transliteration or the way of pronunciation of the paragraph snippet in question by the way of writing you know (in regular alphabetical letters). For example, you can write it "ulul albab", "alhamdulillah", and so on. All personal data will be kept confidential. For more information contact the author via e-mail at the address: m.billyzaelani@gmail.com. Jazakumullah khairan for your participation. Feel free to repeat the audio file if it's unclear, inaudible, or forgot the transliteration.
